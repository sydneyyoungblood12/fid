Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This quick and flavorful spread is always a crowd-pleaser. My father has a great tip:

“Make this the night before so the flavors can blend. Bring it to room temperature before serving. In winter, try it warm!”

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz.) jar sundried tomatoes (preferably packed in oil, drained)
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • ⅔ cup kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 teaspoon capers

Instructions

  1. In a food processor, combine the sundried tomatoes and garlic. Blend until as smooth as possible.
  2. Add the olives and capers. Pulse a few times—just enough to mix them in while keeping some texture.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds. Optional: top with a dollop of goat cheese and a fresh basil leaf for garnish.
